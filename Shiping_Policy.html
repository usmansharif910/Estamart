Shipping Policy

At Esta Mart, we understand that receiving your groceries in a timely and efficient manner is important to you. That’s why we work hard to ensure that your order is delivered as quickly and accurately as possible.

Our delivery options include:

Standard Delivery: Orders placed before 9:00 PM will be delivered within 20-30 min of the order being placed. Delivery bag fees for standard delivery start at 15 PKR/bag and may vary based on the size and weight of your order.

Please note:

We currently only deliver to addresses within Johar Town.

Some items may not be available for delivery due to their size or fragility. In these cases, you’ll have the option to pick up the item at our store or our rider will replace it to your door-step.

We do our best to ensure that all items are in stock, but in the event that an item is out of stock, we will contact you to offer a substitution or issue a refund for the out-of-stock item.

Thank you for choosing Esta Mart for your grocery needs! If you have any questions about our shipping policy, don’t hesitate to reach out to us at info@estamart.com.
